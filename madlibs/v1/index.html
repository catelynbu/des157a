Let's Bake A Cake!

Baking a cake is quite the easy task! First you need to go to the store and buy all of your ingredients. Then, you take them all and throw it into a pan. Mix it well, pour it into a pan, and slam it into the oven! Wait 30 minutes and your oven will ding! Grab your pink frosting, rainbow sprinkles, and get crazy with it. When you’re done, you’ll have a delicious sweet treat.
